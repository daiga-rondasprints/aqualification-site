Seminar programs

Lectures and seminars for swimming coaches.

Sessions can be arranged as a single lecture, a short seminar or a multi-day course. The format is agreed around the group, location and level.

Topics are built for swimming coaches, sports teachers and specialists who work with swimmers at different ages.

Certificate wording and eligibility are confirmed before each programme.

About myself

Dr. Jeļena Solovjova

Sports science doctor, A-category swimming coach and Latvian swimming national team coach since 1989. Professor at the Latvian Academy of Sport Education from 2016 to 2023.

Latvian Olympic swimming team coach in 1996, 2000 and 2004. Continuing-education seminar leader for coaches, author of two textbooks and 50+ scientific publications, with Latvian Science Council expert rights in sports science.

Professional experience

English Channel, 2019.

Professor Jeļena Solovjova's son and athlete, Valērijs Kalmikovs, became the first representative of Latvia to swim the English Channel from Great Britain to France. The official distance is 34 km; with waves and wind, the swim came to 40 km. He completed it overnight from 8 to 9 September 2019, spending 11 hours and 13 minutes in the water.
