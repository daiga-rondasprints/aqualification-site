# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Swimming coaches, sports schools, federations, athlete families and event organisers who need credible swimming education, technique consultation or open-water event information in Latvia.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Dr. Jelena Solovjova's professional background, explains her seminars and consultation work, and gives visitors a direct way to contact her. Success means a reader understands what she can help with without the site overstating certifications, partnerships or institutional affiliations.

## Brand Personality

Careful, experienced, practical. The tone should feel factual and human, with enough warmth to reflect swimming as a lifelong practice.

## Anti-references

Do not make the page feel like a generic AI landing page, a startup sales funnel, or an official partner/certification page for institutions that have not explicitly approved that framing. Avoid inflated claims, invented testimonials, vague marketing slogans and decorative logos used as endorsement signals.

## Design Principles

- Lead with facts Lena recognises: qualifications, years, roles, publications, athletes and real services.
- Prefer direct contact and clear sections over heavy persuasion.
- Keep the turtle/Swimming is Life identity as brand texture, not as a gimmick.
- Use photos and documents as evidence; do not imply affiliations the site cannot prove.
- Keep Latvian as the default public language. English is available only through the top-right language toggle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, readable typography, keyboard-accessible navigation and reduced-motion-safe interactions. No specialised accessibility requirements are known yet.
